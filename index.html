ABOUT ME

Ever since I can remember, I always played games, and while my love for it never changed, my curious nature and hobbies expanded beyond video games. These ranged from Art to science, history to politics to psychology, or from all genres of films to all genres of musics, in order to finally regrouped it all together, into the making of video games.

While I love to talk to people, I also love my freetime exploring the internet and making experiences, reading books, or listening to music.

I often find myself solving puzzles (which is a great birthday gift btw>), or creating things by my hands, thus my first choice to get into an art school, but I became obvious of the limitations of the support compared to all my hobbies and stories I wanted to tell. So, I enrolled into Brassart and started making video games.

While I am captivated and always intrigued by every game and new technology which is released, I am also thrilled to challenge myself to make new concepts which connect, challenge, and intrigued as many players as I can.
